Mowing, Landscaping, and Full-service Lawn Care in Northern Virginia

Established in 2000, Franco American Mowing, Inc. has been providing quality residential lawn mowing and landscaping services to the Northern Virginia area over twenty years!.

We use only small, commercial, 21-inch hand held mowers to eliminate damage from large machinery.

When you hire our company, your lawn will be in good hands.

What is Full-service Lawn Care?

Franco American Mowers provide much more than mowing to our customers. Our full-service plan meets all your lawn care needs. Including lawn clean-up and treatment, seeding, tending plant and flower beds, and maintaining shrubs and bushes, our full-service lawn care runs throughout the year so your lawn looks good whatever the season.

We understand each homeowner's needs are unique. Customers can choose from our services separately or request the full-service lawn maintenance plan.
